Use Anypoint Platform with any of the supported browsers:

. Microsoft Edge with Chromium
. Google Chrome
. Mozilla Firefox
. Safari

MuleSoft supports the latest version of these browsers.

MuleSoft's browser support matche the policy of Salesforce Lightning Experience
with the following exceptions:

. Internet Explorer 11 (IE11) is not supported.
+ IE11 may work with Anypoint Platform, however, you may experience issues.
. Microsoft Edge without Chromium, latest version, is supported through December 31, 2020.
